About the Club

The Edinburgh University Kendo Club was founded in 2001 with the aim of promoting the practice and culture of Kendo within the university. Our club is open to students, staff, and alumni of all skill levels.

We are proud to offer a supportive environment where members can learn Kendo, improve their skills, and participate in competitions.

Our Mission

To foster a community of Kendo practitioners who support each other in their personal and athletic growth. We strive to uphold the values of respect, perseverance, and sportsmanship.
